About Angel

Angel Enciso was born and raised in the South American country of Peru where he and his 9 siblings learned that with a hard work ethic, and faith, nothing is impossible. His father, Jesus, worked for the federal ministry of education in Peru and his mother worked raising and feeding 10 children. Angel attributes all he has to his loving parents, whose sacrifice paved the way for their children to go on and live prosperous lives.

Angel grew up with a love and passion for soccer. He and his brothers would sneak away from home any chance they got to go and play with frineds out in the streets, even with the risk of being caught and chased by their broom-wielding mother. Angel had a bright athletic future ahead of him, but decided to put it on hold to serve and LDS mission.

After his mission, Angel went to live in Sao Paulo, Brazil to puruse his dream of playing professional soccer. Brazil had such an impact on him, that when asked where he is from, Angel responds:

"Made in Peru, but assembled in Brazil."

Not only did Angel's skill and love of soccer grow in Brazil, but in order to make ends meet he began working as a painter. His mentor was a German immigrant named Wenzel Pfeiffer who taught him that quality work is the only kind of work that is worth doing.

After living in Brazil for five years, Angel finally got engaged to the love of his life, an American named Becky Pelo. He decided to retun home to Peru and get married in the Lima, Peru LDS temple. After living in Peru for 1 year after getting married, Angel and Becky decided it was time to move to the United States and begin life there. They currently reside in Orem, UT where Angel continues painting and also manages his own youth soccer club.
